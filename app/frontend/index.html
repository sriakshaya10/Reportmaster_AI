About ReportMaster AI

🤖 What is RAG?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation combines document search with AI to generate accurate, grounded answers from your internal manuals.

📋 How to use

  1. Upload your financial reporting manual (PDF)
  2. Wait for indexing to complete
  3. Ask questions in natural language
  4. Get answers backed by your documents

🔒 Privacy

All documents are stored locally. No data is sent to external servers unless you configure an LLM API key.
